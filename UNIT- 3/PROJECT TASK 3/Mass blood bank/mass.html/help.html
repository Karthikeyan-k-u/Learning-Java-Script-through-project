About Mass Blood Bank

Mass Blood Bank is a digital platform dedicated to bridging the gap between blood donors and those in need. Our mission is to make blood donation easy, transparent, and accessible for everyone.

We connect voluntary donors with hospitals and blood banks in real time, ensuring that life‑saving blood is available when and where it's needed most.

How it works

  • Register as a donor – it takes less than 5 minutes.
  • Schedule appointments at nearby donation centers.
  • Receive alerts when your blood type is urgently needed.
  • Track your donation history and impact on your dashboard.
