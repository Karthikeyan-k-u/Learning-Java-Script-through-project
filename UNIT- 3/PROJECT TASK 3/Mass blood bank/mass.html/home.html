Blood Donation Camp

Event Details

Date: 15 March 2026

Time: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM

Place: Prince Dr.K.Vasudevan College of Engineering and Technology, Main Auditorium

About the Event

Dr. K. Vasudevan College of Engineering and Technology is organizing a Blood Donation Camp to encourage students and staff to donate blood and help save lives. This event focuses on creating awareness about voluntary blood donation and supporting patients in need.

Event Highlights

The blood donation process will be carried out by certified medical professionals in a safe and hygienic environment. Basic health screening will be conducted for all donors before donation. Refreshments will be provided after donation.

Eligibility Criteria for Donors

Age: 18 years and above

Weight: Minimum 50 kg

Must be in good health

Valid ID proof is required
